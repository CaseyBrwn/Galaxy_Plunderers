Galaxy Plunderers
Game Controls
Press Enter to Start the Game
Use the Arrow Keys to Move Left and Right
Fire with Space Bar
Press P to Pause
Press R to Restart
Press M to Mute
Power Ups
Press 1: Extra Life 250 Credits
Press 3: Increase Fire Rate 1000 Credits
Objective
The Deadly Plunderers have come to plunder your galaxy, pilot your ship and fire your laser weapon to destroy the plunderers with out being destroyed yourself!
